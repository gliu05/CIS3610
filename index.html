Big Bear Lake

Four Seasons of Fun!

Doesn't matter if its Summer, Spring, Fall, or Winter.

Fall/Winter - Guests at Big Bear can enjoy a variety of winter activities. Thanks to our Big Bear Mountain and Snow Summit, you can ski down mountains and hills. Can do cool tricks with snowboards. Guest can also enjoy the fun activity of sliding downhills by the means of snow tubing. As well as enjoy the lifts that take you up and down the mountains. Or, guests can take in the sights of the snowy landscapes while snowshoeing and exploring the rare snowy trails on tours that you can’t get at other times of the year.

Summer Activities- At Big Bear, guest can experience multiple fun activities to enjoy summer. Some activies are found at Big Bear Lake. Activities such as, wakeboarding and Waterski lessons. Access to kayaks, paddleboats, canoes, and even jet skis to explore the lake. Fishing is a popular activity at Big Bear Lake and has some of the biggest fish you can find in any lake in southern California. You can even get a tour boat for a more relaxing experience at the lake. Big Bear is also a great place for mountain biking road cycling. Summit Bike Park is Big Bear’s locations for beginners and experienced a like when it comes to biking. It’s filled with twists and turns; as well as fun trails for exploring.
